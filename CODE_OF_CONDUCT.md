# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Nestcore project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior in issues/PRs/discussions

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue tagged `conduct`, or by contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances, up to and including a temporary or permanent ban from the project.
